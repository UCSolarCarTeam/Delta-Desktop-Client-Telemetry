About Us


Glacier Refrigeration is a family owned and operated company. We have been providing superior service to Calgary and area since 1994. We pride ourselves on our knowledge in all areas of the commercial kitchen but our expertise extends beyond that. We service, repair, replace, and maintain most commercial equipment. We offer services for heating, ventilation, air conditioning, refrigeration and commercial equipment. Our dedication to great service means that learning never ends. We are continually upgrading and updating our technicians on the latest equipment, technologies and products in the industry. Give us a call to get more detail on how we can help your business.
